Transgender Cultural Competency
Addressing the unique needs of the transgender client

"Cultural humility is a lifelong process of self-reflection, self-critique and commitment to understanding and respecting different points of view, and engaging with others humbly, authentically and from a place of learning." (Tervalon & Murray-Garcia, 1998).

About US:

The changing landscape of our culture has brought greater recognition and acceptance of transgender and gender non-conforming folks. Due to improved legal protections and better access to healthcare, they are accessing healthcare in increasing numbers, but often, individuals faced with encountering transgender folks are unsure how to proceed.

Transgender folks are seeking compassionate healthcare in settings that provide knowledgable and culturally competent staff in safe and private settings.

We offer consultation and individualized training for you and your staff so everyone can access safe and competent care in a setting that is comfortable and welcoming.
